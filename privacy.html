1. Introduction

Artful Automations LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates hvac.artfulautomation.com and provides AI-powered customer service solutions for HVAC contractors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

We use the information we collect to:
